RoboWar

RoboWar is an advanced Robotics event which will host a fighting arena in the college ground, and two robots would allowed to fight in the arena in the given time slot of 5 mins the rules of the event would be framed in the later stages of preparation of the events. Only electrical robots would be allowed to fight in the event.

TopBot

Topbot will be a robotics event for beginners in the field of robotics and will be mainly comprised of events related to building and programming to a robot to perform a certain task.

Circuit Design

Circuit design event will be a duo challenge of designing Digital/Analog circuits to perform the certain given task. It comprises of both the software and hardware rounds and the team scoring the highest points will win the competition.

Tritechathlon

Triathlon is an interdisciplinary event based consisting of challenges from graphic design, Web/App development & IoT. A group of 3 people make up a team and participate in the same.

Pro Arduino

ProArduino is a hardware competitive event mainly focused on the coming up with the Embedded System solution for real life problem statements and building a working prototype for the same.
