Pro- Arduino

This event will be of 4 hours. Problem statements will be created by the organisers, which will be challenging. In this event, real life problems will be provided to the participants, which have to be solved in stipulated time. Just like coding competitions, all questions will have to be attempted and will carry individual points, and all questions may not carry equal marks. The problems will be divided between easy, medium, and tough. The boards which can be used are arduino and nodemcu. Teams of 2 will be registered. 1st prize will be Rs. 3000, 2nd prize will be 2000, and 3rd prize will be 1000.

Prizes Worth:

Rules

NO controllers other than microcontrollers provided by us are allowed.
Teams of 2 persons are allowed.
Registrations will be done on first-come first serve basis.
Teams will be liable for any damage to any component, and any request for change will not be entertained.
If found using components other than prescribed components, the teams will be straightaway disqualified.
The components will be provided by tesla, and only those components have to be used throughout the course of the event.
